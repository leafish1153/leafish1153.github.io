Truth and Reconciliation Commission

Call to Action #76:
Supporting Indigenous communities in locating, documenting, and memorializing children who died while attending residential schools.

This call to action recognizes the profound loss caused by residential schools and emphasizes remembrance, accountability, and respect for Indigenous children whose lives were taken and forgotten.

Research: Missing & Murdered Indigenous Women and Girls

What?

Disproportionate violence faced by Indigenous women due to systemic racism and colonial legacies.

Who?

Indigenous women and girls, Indigenous communities, advocacy groups, and governments.

Where?

Primarily Canada, with similar issues in the United States and Australia.

When?

An ongoing crisis for decades, gaining major attention in recent years.

Why?

Colonial policies created long-term systemic discrimination and injustice.
